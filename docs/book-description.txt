Discover treasured family recipes, lovingly transcribed from handwritten recipe cards collected over generations. From comforting soups and hearty entrees to decadent desserts and holiday favorites, this cookbook brings together the best of American home cooking. Each recipe includes a photo of the original handwritten recipe card.

Inside you'll find:
- Appetizers for entertaining
- Breakfast favorites
- Hearty entrees
- Warming soups
- Fresh salads
- Vegetables & sides
- Dozens of desserts, cookies & sweet treats
- Cakes & breads
- A complete Thanksgiving menu
- Refreshing drinks & more

These tried-and-true recipes will become your new family favorites.
