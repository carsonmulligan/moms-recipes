## APPETIZERS

### Bruschetta

**Ingredients:**
- 2-3 medium ripe tomatoes, coarsely chopped
- 4 Tbsp olive oil, divided
- Sliced Tuscan bread
- 2 cloves garlic, halved
- Sea salt
- Coarsely chopped fresh basil

**Instructions:**
1. Combine tomatoes and half of the olive oil in bowl and toss.
2. Marinate for 10 mins at room temp.
3. Toast the bread slices.
4. Gently rub the toasted bread with the cut end of raw garlic cloves.
5. Top each slice with tomatoes.
6. Sprinkle with salt and chopped basil, drizzle remaining olive oil.

### Deviled Eggs
**Yields:** 24 deviled egg halves

**Ingredients:**
- 12 large eggs
- 1/2 c mayonnaise
- 2 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
- 1 Tbsp yellow mustard
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- paprika for garnish

**Instructions:**
Place eggs in single layer in saucepan. Cover with water. Bring to boil over high heat. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand 12 minutes. Drain and place in ice water until cool. Peel eggs and slice in half lengthwise. Remove yolks and place in bowl. Mash yolks with fork. Add mayonnaise, relish, mustard, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Spoon or pipe filling into egg white halves. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.

## BREAKFAST

### French Toast Bake
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Bake Time:** 45 minutes
**Servings:** 6-8

**Ingredients:**
- 1 loaf French bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 8 large eggs
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 Tbsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- Pinch of salt

**Topping:**
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup cold butter, cut into pieces

**Instructions:**
1. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place bread cubes in dish.
2. In large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Pour over bread cubes. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
3. Preheat oven to 350°F.
4. For topping: combine flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over bread mixture.
5. Bake 45 minutes or until golden brown and center is set. Serve with maple syrup.

## SOUPS

### Baked Potato Soup

**Ingredients:**
- 4 large baking potatoes
- 2/3 cup butter
- 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 6 cups milk
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 4 green onions, chopped
- 12 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
- 1 1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup sour cream

**Instructions:**
1. Bake potatoes at 400°F for 1 hour or until done. Let cool.
2. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise; scoop out pulp and set aside.
3. In large Dutch oven, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour, stirring until smooth. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
4. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is thickened and bubbly.
5. Add potato pulp, salt, pepper, 2 Tbsp green onions, 1/2 cup bacon, and 1 cup cheese. Cook until thoroughly heated.
6. Stir in sour cream. Add extra milk if needed for desired thickness.
7. Serve topped with remaining green onions, bacon, and cheese.

## SALADS

### Cucumber Mango Salad
**Servings:** 4-6

**Ingredients:**
- 2 English cucumbers, diced
- 2 ripe mangoes, diced
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 Tbsp lime juice
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. In large bowl, combine cucumbers, mangoes, and cilantro.
2. In small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
3. Pour dressing over cucumber mixture and toss to combine.
4. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving to allow flavors to blend.
5. Serve chilled.

## ENTREES

### Salmon Tacos
**Servings:** 4

**Ingredients:**
**For the Salmon:**
- 1.5 lbs salmon fillets
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

**For the Slaw:**
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 2 Tbsp lime juice
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- Salt to taste

**For Serving:**
- 8 small flour or corn tortillas
- Avocado slices
- Lime wedges
- Sour cream

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Rub salmon with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Bake 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily with fork.
4. Meanwhile, make slaw: combine cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil, and salt in bowl. Toss well.
5. Flake salmon into large pieces.
6. Warm tortillas.
7. Assemble tacos with salmon, slaw, avocado, and sour cream. Serve with lime wedges.

## VEGETABLES & SIDES

### Garlic Roasted Potato Wedges
**Servings:** 6

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs russet potatoes, cut into wedges
- 3 Tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line large baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In large bowl, combine olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
3. Add potato wedges and toss until evenly coated.
4. Arrange potatoes in single layer on prepared baking sheet.
5. Roast 35-40 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
6. Sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.

## CAKES

### Hisae's Choc Cake
**Source:** Hisae (family recipe)

**Ingredients:**
**Cake:**
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup cocoa powder
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup strong black coffee, cooled
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

**Frosting:**
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 2/3 cup cocoa powder
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
2. In large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add eggs, coffee, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
4. Pour batter into prepared pans.
5. Bake 30-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
6. Cool in pans 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
7. For frosting: beat butter and cocoa together. Alternately add powdered sugar and milk, beating until smooth and spreadable. Stir in vanilla.
8. Frost cooled cake.

## DESSERTS

### Apple Pie
**Servings:** 8

**Ingredients:**
**Crust:**
- 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 cup cold butter, cut into cubes
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

**Filling:**
- 6-7 cups apples, peeled and sliced (Granny Smith or mix)
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp butter, cut into small pieces
- 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

**Instructions:**
1. For crust: combine flour, salt, and sugar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add ice water, 1 Tbsp at a time, until dough comes together. Divide in half, form into disks, wrap, and refrigerate 1 hour.
2. Preheat oven to 425°F.
3. For filling: combine apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in large bowl.
4. Roll out one disk of dough and fit into 9-inch pie plate. Fill with apple mixture. Dot with butter.
5. Roll out second disk of dough and place over filling. Trim, seal, and crimp edges. Cut slits in top for steam to escape. Brush with beaten egg.
6. Bake 45-50 minutes until crust is golden and filling is bubbly. Cover edges with foil if browning too quickly.
7. Cool on wire rack at least 2 hours before serving.

## DRINKS

### Pumpkin Spiced Latte
**Servings:** 2

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups whole milk
- 2 Tbsp pumpkin puree
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee or 2 shots espresso
- Whipped cream for topping
- Additional cinnamon for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. In small saucepan, combine milk, pumpkin puree, sugar, vanilla, pumpkin pie spice, and cinnamon.
2. Heat over medium heat, whisking constantly, until hot but not boiling.
3. Remove from heat and whisk vigorously until frothy, or use milk frother.
4. Divide coffee between two mugs.
5. Pour pumpkin milk mixture over coffee.
6. Top with whipped cream and sprinkle with cinnamon.
7. Serve immediately.

## THANKSGIVING

### Sweet Potato Casserole
**Servings:** 8-10

**Ingredients:**
**Sweet Potatoes:**
- 4 lbs sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp salt

**Topping:**
- 1 cup brown sugar, packed
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup butter, melted
- 1 cup chopped pecans

**Instructions:**
1. Peel and cube sweet potatoes. Boil until tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain well.
2. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. Mash sweet potatoes. Add butter, sugar, milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt. Mix well.
4. Spread mixture in prepared baking dish.
5. For topping: combine brown sugar, flour, melted butter, and pecans. Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture.
6. Bake 25-30 minutes until topping is golden brown.

### Pumpkin Pie
**Servings:** 8

**Ingredients:**
- 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
- 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 (9-inch) unbaked pie crust

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. In large bowl, whisk together pumpkin, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt until smooth.
3. Pour into pie crust.
4. Bake 15 minutes at 425°F.
5. Reduce temperature to 350°F and continue baking 35-40 minutes, until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
6. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Refrigerate until serving.
7. Serve with whipped cream.

## MISCELLANEOUS

### Snickerdoodles
**Yields:** About 4 dozen cookies

**Ingredients:**
**Cookies:**
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp cream of tartar
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt

**Coating:**
- 3 Tbsp sugar
- 3 tsp ground cinnamon

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. In large bowl, cream butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs.
3. In separate bowl, combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to butter mixture.
4. In small bowl, mix 3 Tbsp sugar and cinnamon for coating.
5. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in cinnamon-sugar mixture.
6. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
7. Bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned but still soft. Centers will puff up during baking then flatten.
8. Cool on baking sheets 2 minutes, then remove to wire racks.

### Paula Deen's Banana Pudding
**Source:** Paula Deen
**Servings:** 10-12

**Ingredients:**
- 2 bags Pepperidge Farm Chessmen cookies
- 6-8 bananas, sliced
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 (5 oz) box instant French vanilla pudding
- 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 (12 oz) container Cool Whip, thawed

**Instructions:**
1. Line bottom of 9x13 inch dish with cookies.
2. In mixing bowl, combine milk and pudding mix; beat until smooth.
3. In another bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add condensed milk and beat until smooth.
4. Fold Cool Whip into cream cheese mixture.
5. Add pudding mixture to cream cheese mixture and stir until well blended.
6. Pour half of mixture over cookies. Layer with half of sliced bananas.
7. Add another layer of cookies, then remaining pudding mixture.
8. Top with remaining bananas and crush remaining cookies over top.
9. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

### German Pancakes in the Oven
**Servings:** 4-6

**Ingredients:**
- 6 large eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole milk
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup butter
- Powdered sugar for dusting
- Fresh lemon juice
- Maple syrup (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place butter in 9x13 inch baking dish and put in oven to melt.
2. In blender, combine eggs, flour, milk, and salt. Blend until smooth.
3. Remove hot baking dish from oven. Tilt to coat bottom with melted butter.
4. Pour batter into hot buttered dish.
5. Bake 20-25 minutes until puffed and golden brown.
6. Remove from oven (it will deflate). Cut into squares.
7. Serve immediately dusted with powdered sugar and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Maple syrup optional.
